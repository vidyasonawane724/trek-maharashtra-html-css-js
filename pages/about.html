Why Choose Trek Maharashtra?

Maharashtra Trekking Portal helps trekking lovers explore the beautiful forts, mountains and historical locations of Maharashtra.

  • Detailed Fort Information
    Get complete information about famous forts like Rajgad, Torna, Raigad and Sinhagad.
  • Direct Trek Planning
    Plan trekking journeys easily with route guidance and travel information.
  • Accurate Location Data
    Find exact locations of trekking forts and nearby travel routes.
  • Adventure Experience
    Explore historical forts while enjoying adventure trekking.

Famous Forts Timeline

1647 – Torna Fort

First fort captured by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

1656 – Rajgad Fort

Capital fort of the Maratha Empire.

1674 – Raigad Fort

Coronation place of Shivaji Maharaj.

1818 – Sinhagad Fort

Historic battle of Tanaji Malusare.
